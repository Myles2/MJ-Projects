Chris Pratt

A man who changed the way we look at the galaxy.

Chris Pratt in his character role as Star Lord.

This is some brief history of how Chris Pratt became the iconic character we know as Star Lord:

  • Born on June 21, 1979 in Virginia, Minnesota
  • Chris Pratt was discovered by actress/director Rae Dawn Chong and eventually established a successful TV career
  • Starring in series like Everwood, The O.C. and Parks and Recreation.
  • He ventured onto the big screen as well, nabbing supporting roles in Wanted, Moneyball and Zero Dark Thirty
  • Before enjoying huge box office success as the lead actor in the Guardians of the Galaxy and Jurassic World franchises.
  • Pratt plays Peter Quill, introduced as a space-faring scavenger who also goes by the name Star Lord. Though focusing on comic characters not previously widely known, the film earned more than $770 million worldwide, leading to the release of the even more profitable Guardians of the Galaxy 2 (2017) and the inclusion of its characters in other Marvel films, like Avengers: Infinity War (2018).
